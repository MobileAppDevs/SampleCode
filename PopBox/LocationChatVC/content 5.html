About the program

The Popbox Ambassador Program is our way of saying thank you to you, our regular users, and to offer you a way of staying engaged with our brand.

We take a limited number of ambassadors at each university and college. As ambassadors, you will be our valued representatives at your campuses, raising awareness and membership, and maintaining a positive culture in your campus group chats.

The program is a 12-week voluntary internship, which we are happy to extend further for our successful ambassadors.

The role of a Popbox ambassador

As a Popbox ambassador, you will unleash your incredible energy, positivity, proactivity and resilience to:
  • Increase the membership in your campus
  • Make new and existing members feel welcome, and enjoy a positive and friendly chat group
  • Proactively identify ways to improve your campus chat and the broader Popbox experience

Why should you become a Popbox ambassador?

Our ambassadors are very important to us, and we do everything we can to give you an enriching experience.
So why should you become a Popbox ambassador? Here are just a few reasons.
  • You will get valuable experience, which will position you well for employment opportunities after graduation. We can give references!
  • Be recognised on campus. On your campus chat group, you will have an exclusive badge next to your name, to identify you as one of our valued ambassadors
  • Every year we seek to hire some of our top graduating ambassadors in our teams globally
  • Be part of an exclusive ‘Ambassadors chat’ where you can network with other ambassadors and future leaders
  • An opportunity to be showcased on our Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Google+ pages. Make sure you’re following us on those pages!

What we are looking for

We are looking for students who..
  • Believe in the Popbox philosophy and want to expand its presence on campus by acquiring users
  • Are energetic, dynamic, proactive and have a positive mindset
  • Are persistent and tough, and will keep going when things don’t go as planned (happens to all of us!)
  • Are seeking high-quality work experience and possible career opportunities
  • Want to have lots of fun!
